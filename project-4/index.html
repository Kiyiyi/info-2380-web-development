Our Programs

How We Help Eastie

Community Sustained Agriculture

Eastie Farm's CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) is a program for Eastie residents to receive fresh, locally-grown produce.

Produce For People

Eastie Farm offers food support for those in need & we also have resources for other aid.

K-12 Teen Programs

Engaging educational programs tailored for students & fostering a love for nature, while helping out their community.

Volunteer Work

Outdoor adventures and learning experiences for volunteers, connecting them with nature and supporting the local community.
