BACKGROUND

I am an entrepreneur based out of East Boston, MA. I am a Boston native and I have been coding for 2 years now, but very recently started a web development company. It was a natural choice for me because I have a passion for business and I have always been interested in technology & everytime I'm able to help others, it has never felt like work to me.

On a longer time horizon, I want to grow the work that I am doing now to be able to help out others. From many past and current owners, the growth of tech giants has cut out businesses that aren't able to adapt to new conditions. Newer companies also have a hard time sprouting into worthwhile investments because of the harsh competitions.

Because I felt restlessness and unhappiness being an employee, I would love to help inspire others who feel the same to start something of their own. In the future, I strive to give anyone with an internet connection open access to free classes, tools, resources, and connections to mentors to start something that can help out their community.
